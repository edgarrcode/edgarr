Dell Ratings & Reviews Redesign

While working with Bazaarvoice I had the opportunity to redesign the Ratings & Reviews implementation. This redesign consisted in two parts, their normal theme, and a dark theme used for the Alienware products. The redesign consisted in updating some elements to match their new look. The client provided mockups and I used CSS match the mockups.

It was important to follow their style guide to match every element like buttons, links & headings.
